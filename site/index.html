Services

Picturing a beautiful new hardscape or paved patio in your home’s outdoor space? LoMar can help! We can answer questions about our hardscaping services, discuss your vision for your outdoor living area, and give you a price quote. At LoMar, we are always happy to help both current and potential clients. You can fill out our form to request a free initial consultation with one of our hardscaping experts.

Outdoor Living


Outdoor Living areas include any space outside of the home that effectively acts as additional square footage. These spaces are what we specialize in building, often featuring custom stonework and paving. Outdoor Living areas can be patios, fire pits, wood-burning fire places, grill islands, outdoor kitchen areas, seating areas with wall seats and benches, multi-level terraces, and more.


Fireplaces, firepits & grills


An outdoor fireplace is a great addition to any outdoor living space. It creates a focal point in outdoor spaces. An outdoor fireplace can also extend your enjoyment of outdoor spaces into the fall and winter months, with outdoor grill islands providing a central point of food and fun. Fire pits also create a natural addition to any patio hardscape, offering a welcoming place for family members and guests to gather to chat, tell stories, and roast marshmallows.

Driveways & entraces


Our custom driveway design and paving boosts curb appeal of homes, seamlessly tying together outside living space elements and the rest of the house. Our paved driveways require little maintenance and can bear more weight than many concrete driveways, never cracking with thanks to the way stones flex to accommodate pressure and weight. If settling or displacement occurs in our natural paving stones, the paved driveway can be easily repaired by our experts at LoMar.

Custom stone veneers


Stone veneer creates a home exterior and outdoor living space that offers more curb appeal to passersby, something fitting of any modern home with a desire to host and entertain. LoMar specializes in stonework and hardscaping, with added focus on veneer installs. Stone veneer is the use of natural stones to cover a structure for decorative or protective purposes, with each stone averaging a thickness of one inch. A stone veneer can give a homes exterior a new look and feel.


Retaining wall & gardens


Resolving sloping lawns and uneven yards can be done with construction of a retaining wall. LoMar designs and installs retaining walls to deal with elevation changes and uneven slopes in outdoor areas, serving as a functional and structurally-sound solution to an off-camber living space. Our retaining wall construction services have been used to level and beautify many outdoor areas, such as pool decks , gardens, patios, and fire pits . Retaining walls hold back earth and create usable, flat areas, and have many applications.

Patios, pool decks & pathways


Extend your living space into the outdoors with a beautifully-paved patio design by LoMar. Our team has some of the best patio, walkway, and pool deck builders in the Raleigh and Northern Virginia areas, capable of turning your vision into a reality. Our patio construction enables you to entertain, relax, garden, grill, or just spend time outside on a warm day. Our custom-designed patios are suited perfectly to your space, built to fit the specifications provided. We use beautiful, quality materials to create a durable, low-maintenance patio that will last for years to come.
