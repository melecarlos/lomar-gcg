Custom stone veneers
Stone veneer creates a home exterior and outdoor living space that offers more curb appeal to passersby, something fitting of any modern home with a desire to host and entertain. COVIS specializes in stonework and hardscaping, with added focus on veneer installs.
Stone veneer is the use of natural stones to cover a structure for decorative or protective purposes, with each stone averaging a thickness of one inch. A stone veneer can give a home’s exterior a new look and feel.
