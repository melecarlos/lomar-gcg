Retaining wall & gardens 
Resolving sloping lawns and uneven yards can be done with construction of a retaining wall. LoMar designs and installs retaining walls to deal with elevation changes and uneven slopes in outdoor areas, serving as a functional and structurally-sound solution to an off-camber living space.
Our retaining wall construction services have been used to level and beautify many outdoor areas, such as pool decks , gardens, patios, and fire pits . Retaining walls hold back earth and create usable, flat areas, and have many applications.
